## About Us

**Intiqaal Information Technology** is a Riyadh-based technology company specializing in digital transformation and enterprise software solutions. We help organizations across diverse sectors modernize their operations through a focused suite of services — from ERP implementation to cloud infrastructure and AI-driven analytics.

We serve clients in **government, energy, finance, healthcare, and education**, partnering with them to improve performance, increase profitability, and streamline business processes through modern management systems.
